# ZenSmash — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 12, 2026*

ZenSmash is a stress-relief game made by Mikulas Frenak.

## The short version

**ZenSmash collects no data. None.**

## The full version

- **No data collection.** The app does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, or identifiers.
- **No accounts.** There is nothing to sign up for.
- **No internet access required.** The game runs entirely on your device and works offline.
- **No third-party services.** No analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no tracking of any kind.
- **Local settings only.** Your preferences (sound, haptics, particles, language) and collected in-game treasures are stored only on your device and never leave it. Deleting the app deletes them.
- **Children.** The app is suitable for all ages and, because it collects nothing, is compliant with COPPA/GDPR-K by design.
