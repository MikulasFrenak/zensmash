## App Store / Google Play — English

**Name:** ZenSmash
**Subtitle (iOS, ≤30 chars):** Smash cubes. Find calm.
**Short description (Android, ≤80 chars):** Smash cubes, build a rainbow, bloom a lotus. Leave calmer than you came. 🌈

**Description:**

Stressed? Smash something. 🌱

ZenSmash is a tiny world where breaking things makes everything bloom. Tap glassy green cubes and watch them crack exactly where you hit, chip, squash and finally shatter into rainbow bursts — with soft haptics and gentle music-box melodies (a different tune every time).

Every break rewards you:
🎁 Free hidden surprises trapped in the cubes — collect them all
☁️ Or catch a joke floating by on a little cloud, in your language

And as your stress leaves, the world wakes up:
🌈 A rainbow fills with color, stripe by stripe
🪷 A lotus mandala blooms petal by petal behind the cubes
🌸 Meadow flowers open, fireflies appear, sometimes it gently rains
🐢 Little animals drop by just to wave at you

After two full fields of cubes, the mandala shines, a chord blooms, and you get your results — plus a rhyme to take with you. Then you can go again. Or not. There's no pressure here.

No scores. No timers. No ads. No fail state. No accounts, ever.
Just you, some very smashable cubes, and a world that's happy you came.
